Ok. GTDify iGoogle gadget is up and running.

Follow the steps in blog post to add this gadget in your iGoogle page:
http://www.gtdify.com/2009/07/02/gtdify-igoogle-gadget/

For Tracks developers:
Implementation is same as Gmail gadget, except you have to submit your gadget to Google for approval. Without approval you can’t add your gadget in iGoogle page.

First, Navigate the following Url to check the content of this Xml file:
http://www.gtdify.com/modules/igoogle/index.xml
Change the fields in Xml with respective values and upload to any public folder of your server.

After uploading this xml, submit this gadget to Google through following link and wait for their approval:
http://www.google.com/ig/submit

Once they approve your gadget, you can search this gadget and add it in your iGoogle page.
